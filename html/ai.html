BSc Artificial Intelligence

Build intelligent machines and systems

Course Overview

Artificial Intelligence studies how computers can learn, think, and solve problems like humans. It helps machines understand information, recognize patterns, and make decisions based on data.

AI is used in many everyday technologies such as voice assistants, recommendation systems, and smart devices. It continues to grow and plays an important role in making technology more helpful and efficient in our daily lives.

Main Subjects

  • Machine Learning
  • Neural Networks
  • Robotics
  • Natural Language Processing
