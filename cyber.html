BSc Cyber Security

Protect systems from cyber threats

Course Overview

This course teaches students how to protect computer systems and networks from cyber attacks and data breaches. It covers the basics of online security and safe practices.

Students learn how to identify threats, prevent attacks, and keep data secure. These skills are important for protecting information and maintaining trust in today’s digital world.

Main Subjects

  • Network Security
  • Ethical Hacking
  • Digital Forensics
  • Cryptography
